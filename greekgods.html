At the heart of Greek mythology lies the pantheon of deities who were said to reside on Mount Olympus, Greece’s tallest mountain. The Greek Gods of Olympus reigned over every aspect of human life from their perch. The olympian gods and goddesses resembled men and women (though they could transform into animals and other forms) and were prone to human flaws and desires, according to several tales.
